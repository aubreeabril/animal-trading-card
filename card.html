Cat

A genetic study in 2007 concluded that all domestic cats are descended from Near Eastern wildcats, having diverged around 8,000 BC in the Middle East. (NYTimes)

  • Scientific Name: Felis silvestris catus
  • Average Length: 18 inches
  • Average Lifespan: 4-5 years
  • Habitat: Cats can live almost anywhere. The only continent they don't live on is Antarctica.

Cats come in a wide variety and photos of them on the internet bring joy.
